## Overview

As Head of Ecosystem at [BlockApps](https://blockapps.net), Bob is helping to drive the transition of STRATO from a closed-source platform to a rich, decentralized ecosystem, bridging on-chain and real world assets.

He is also driving the [Early Days of Ethereum](https://earlydaysofeth.org) history project, with extensive content and ongoing [conversations series](https://earlydaysofeth.org/conversations).

He regularly [speaks at conferences](https://bobsummerwill.com/schedule/).


## Prior lives

- Executive Director for the [ETC Cooperative](https://etccooperative.org/) from 2019 to 2024
- Organized [Proof of Work Summit](https://powsummit.com) conferences in 2023 and 2024, and [ETC Summit](https://web.archive.org/web/20190519100846/https://etcsummit.com/) in 2019
- Worked full-time on the launch and first year of the [Enterprise Ethereum Alliance](http://entethalliance.org) in 2016-2017 while working at [Consensys](https://consensys.io)
- Various [Hyperledger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperledger) activities on-and-off since 2016
- Core developer on [cpp-ethereum](https://github.com/ethereum/aleth/tree/ce740fbdfafea24541a0bf78d80db2478cf02a86) at the [Ethereum Foundation](https://www.earlydaysofeth.org/articles/stiftung-ethereum/) in 2016
- [cpp-ethereum-cross](https://github.com/doublethinkco/cpp-ethereum-cross) ARM Linux cross-builds for resource-constrained devices in 2015-2016
- [Smartwatch developer](https://web.archive.org/web/20201023034515/https://www.iotgadgets.com/2015/12/how-to-deploy-to-gear-s2-smartwatch/) in 2015
- [MonoTizen](https://kitsilanosoftware.github.io/MonoTizen/) port of Mono tooling to [Tizen](https://kitsilanosoftware.wordpress.com/2014/08/13/the-tizen-project-is-broken-we-will-be-spending-some-time-apart-3/) mobile Linux (and later [Sailfish OS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sailfish_OS)) in 2014
- Eighteen years and [20 AAA games (6 x FIFA)](https://bobsummerwill.com/bobliography/) in the games industry ([Psygnosis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psygnosis), [Electronic Arts](https://ea.com), [Roadhouse Interactive](https://web.archive.org/web/20141221102653/http://www.roadhouseinteractive.com/), [DeNA](https://dena.com/intl/) and [Sony](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sony_Interactive_Entertainment)) from 1996 to 2014
- Programming since 1984, as a ten year old - BBC Micro, ZX Spectrum, Commodore 64, PS1, PS2, PS3, PS4, XBOX, XBOX360, XBOX One, Gamecube, Android, iOS, macOS, Windows and Linux.
- Originally from the UK, he has lived in Vancouver since 2003, and is now a dual citizen of Canada and the UK.
